# Mock Risk Control Policy

This document is simulated for learning and project demonstration. It is not a real bank policy.

- Debt-to-income ratio is a key repayment-capacity indicator.
- More overdue records should reduce the mock risk score.
- A requested amount greater than twelve months of income requires stricter manual review.
- High-risk customers must be reviewed by a human reviewer before any final business decision.
- Asset proof may improve confidence but cannot replace repayment and compliance checks.
