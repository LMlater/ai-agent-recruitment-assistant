# Mock Personal Credit Policy

This document is simulated for learning and project demonstration. It is not a real bank policy.

- Applicants should be adults with stable and verifiable income.
- Income proof should be reviewed before a manual approval decision is made.
- Requested amount should remain proportionate to monthly income and repayment capacity.
- AI must not automatically approve or reject personal credit applications.
- Approval conclusions must be confirmed by a human reviewer and retained in audit records.
